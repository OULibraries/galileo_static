The Schusterman Library is the library for students, staff and faculty of Tulsa-based programs from both the OU-Norman academic campus and the Health Sciences Center. The library housed the exhibit "The Scientific Revolution" that explored the controversial claim that “mathematics is the language of nature.” An opening reception and Star Party were held as part of this exhibit.

 


Exhibits

The Scientific Revolution
Aug 24, 2015 - Dec 18, 2015

When Galileo announced that “mathematics is the language of nature,” he was making a then-controversial claim about how nature is best known and understood.
